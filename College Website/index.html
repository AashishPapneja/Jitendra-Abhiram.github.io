Admission Process

Courses Offered

The admissions to different courses of IIITL are offered through the following exams.

B.Tech

The admissions to B.TECH programmes of IIITL is offered through the national exam Joint Entrance Exam (JEE - MAINS). One has to Qualify the JEE-Mains and appear for JOSAA / CSAB counselling for admission based on merit.
Please refer NTA for examination updates and more details.

M.Tech

The admissions to M.TECH programmes of IIITL is offered through the national exam GATE. One has to Qualify the GATE and appear for counselling for admission based on merit.
Please refer GATE for examination updates and more details.

MBA

The admissions to M.TECH programmes of IIITL is offered through the national exam CAT. One has to Qualify the CAT and appear for counselling for admission based on merit.
Please refer CAT for examination updates and more details.
